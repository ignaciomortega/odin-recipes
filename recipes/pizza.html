Pizza

Description

This is the recipe for a delicious homemade pizza, you don't have to be a cheff to achieve the greatness of pizza, just follow the instructions bellow and get ready to enter paradise

Ingredients

  • Self-rising Flour 250ml
  • Water 170ml
  • Olive Oil 50ml
  • Salt to taste
  • Tomato puree 200gr
  • Mozzarella 200gr

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 200Â°C.
  2. In a bowl mix the flour, the water, the oil, and add the salt to it. Knead it until you have an homogeneous mass
  3. Extend the pizza mass on a baking dish and put it in the oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Take it out and add the tomato puree and the cheese.
  5. Put it back in the oven for 10-15 minutes more and you have your pizza done.
  6. Enjoy it!
